# Problem Statement

India has over 300M+ individuals with limited or no formal credit history.

Traditional systems rely on:

* Salary slips 
* Bank statements
* Credit history

This excludes:

* Gig workers
* Daily wage earners
* Informal sector participants

## Opportunity

Use alternative data + AI to assess:

* Behavioral consistency
* Financial discipline proxies
* Stability signals

## Vision

Build a parallel credit system that is:

* Inclusive
* Real-time
* Behavior-driven
